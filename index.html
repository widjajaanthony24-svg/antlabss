How it works

Three steps. Zero effort.

01
Capture
AntLabs watches your conversations across all AI tools using a MutationObserver on the page DOM. Every question and answer is saved locally to your device, silently.
02
Score
Your Passport Score measures the depth of your memory across 7 dimensions — days active, topics covered, LLMs used, hours tracked, and workflow habits. It grows as you learn.
03
Inject
When you open any AI tool, AntLabs scores your cards by topic relevance and secretly prepends the most relevant context to your message. The LLM knows your history — you typed nothing extra.
